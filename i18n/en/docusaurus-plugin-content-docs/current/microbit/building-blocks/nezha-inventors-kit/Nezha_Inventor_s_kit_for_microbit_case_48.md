### Step 1

![](https://wiki-media-ef.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/i18n/en/docusaurus-plugin-content-docs/current/microbit/building-blocks/nezha-inventors-kit/images/neza-inventor-s-kit-case-37-04.png)
