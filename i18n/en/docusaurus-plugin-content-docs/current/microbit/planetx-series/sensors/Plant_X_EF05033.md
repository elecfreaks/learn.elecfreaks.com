### Step 1

Go to  [Python editor](https://python.microbit.org/v/2.0)

![](https://wiki-media-ef.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/i18n/en/docusaurus-plugin-content-docs/current/microbit/sensor/planet-x-sensors/images/05001_07.png)
